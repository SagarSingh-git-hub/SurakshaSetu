How It Works

Three steps to cleaner communities

From spotting an issue to full resolution — Suraksha Setu makes civic action effortless.

📍
01

Spot & Locate

Notice a problem nearby? Your GPS auto-captures the exact location instantly. No manual entry — or drop a pin on the interactive map if GPS is unavailable.

📸
02

Snap & Submit

Upload photos directly from your camera or gallery. Our AI instantly analyses your images — auto-tagging the issue type and assigning a priority score. No account needed.

🤝
03

NGOs Take Action

Partner NGOs and volunteers see all reports in real-time on their dashboard. They plan drives, dispatch teams, and update the community as issues get resolved.

How do I submit a new environmental issue report?
Click the green "+ Report Issue" button in the top navigation bar. Fill in the category (Garbage, Plastic, Water, etc.), add a photo or description, pin the location on the map, and set the priority. Your report will be visible on the live map immediately after submission.
Why is my report showing "In Progress" but no update from the team?
In Progress means a moderator or field officer has picked up the issue for review. Resolution timelines depend on priority — High priority issues are typically resolved within 24–48 hours. You can track status in real time from your report detail page.
How do I access the Admin Dashboard?
The Admin Dashboard is accessible only to Super Admins and Moderators. Click the "Admin" button in the top navigation. If the button is not visible, your account does not have the required role. Contact your Super Admin to request elevated access.
How do I download or share a community certificate?
Go to Certificate → View Certificates, find your certificate by name or ID, and click the download icon to save as PDF. Each certificate includes a QR code for instant verification. You can also resend it to your registered email from the same page.
Still need help?
Our support team is available Mon–Sat, 9 AM – 6 PM IST. Average response time: under 2 hours.

How do I submit a new environmental issue report?
Click "+ Report Issue" in the top nav. Select the issue category, upload a photo, pin your location on the map, write a brief description, and set the priority level. Submit — your report goes live on the map instantly and notifies the nearest moderator.
Can I report anonymously?
Anonymous reporting can be enabled or disabled by the platform admin. If enabled, your name won't appear on the public report, but your account is still logged internally for moderation purposes.
How do I track the status of my report?
Go to the Community Feed or your profile and find your submitted report. Status updates (Open → In Progress → Resolved) are shown in real time. You'll also receive email notifications if email alerts are enabled in your settings.
How do I export all reports as CSV or PDF?
In the Admin Dashboard, click "Export PDF" for the current view, or go to Reports & Exports → select date range and filters → click "Export CSV". Data includes all report fields: ID, category, location, priority, status, and timestamps.
How do I change a report's priority?
Open the report from the Live Map or Active Issues list, click the priority badge, and select the new level (Low / Medium / High). Moderators can escalate; only Super Admins can downgrade High priority issues.
How do I reset my password?
Click "Forgot password" on the login page and enter your registered email. You'll receive a password reset link valid for 1 hour. If you don't receive the email within 5 minutes, check your spam folder or contact support.
The live map is not loading — what should I do?
First, check your internet connection. The live map uses OpenStreetMap/Leaflet which requires an active connection. Try a hard refresh (Ctrl+Shift+R). If the issue persists, clear browser cache or try a different browser. Contact support if the problem continues.
